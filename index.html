About Us

Dinerhive began as a community-focused savings cooperative. Today, we've grown into a full-service financial institution serving over 200,000 customers across Nigeria. Our evolution from a modest savings platform to offering loans, solar financing, and retail solutions reflects our commitment to solving real financial challenges with innovative products.

At Dinerhive, we bridge traditional financial security with modern convenience. Our unique integration of solar financing with savings programs – where customers can allocate savings toward renewable energy investments – has empowered over 15,000 households to reduce energy costs by 40% while building financial assets.
